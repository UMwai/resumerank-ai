**Founded:** 2025
**Stage:** Pre-Seed / Bootstrapped

## The Problem

Staffing agencies and recruitment firms waste 15-20 hours per week manually reviewing resumes. With an average of 250 applications per job posting, recruiters spend 80% of their time on unqualified candidates and only 20% talking to viable prospects.

**Pain Points:**
- Manual resume screening costs $240,000/year for a 10-person recruiting team
- 67% of resumes don't match basic job requirements but still require review
- Qualified candidates are missed due to resume format variations
- No standardized scoring leads to inconsistent hiring decisions
- High-volume recruiting (warehouse, healthcare, hospitality) is unsustainable manually

## The Solution

ResumeRank AI is an **AI-powered resume screening API** that automatically parses, analyzes, and ranks candidates against job descriptions in under 3 seconds per resume.

**Core Features:**
1. **Instant Resume Parsing** - Extract structured data from any PDF/DOCX resume
2. **AI-Powered Matching** - Score candidates 0-100 based on job requirements
3. **Intelligent Insights** - Generate 2-3 sentence screening notes explaining the match
4. **Batch Processing** - Upload 100+ resumes, get ranked spreadsheet in minutes
5. **Interview Question Generator** - Auto-generate role-specific questions for top candidates

**Differentiation:**
- **Industry-Specific Models**: Pre-trained for healthcare, IT, warehouse/logistics (vs. generic tools)
- **Transparent Pricing**: $0.75/resume or $149-299/month (vs. $299-14,995/year competitors)
- **Zero Integration Required**: Upload interface + API for technical users
- **Compliance-First**: Human-in-loop positioning, bias disclaimers, GDPR-ready

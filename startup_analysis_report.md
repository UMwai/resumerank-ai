## Comparative Analysis & Recommendations

### Ranking by Market Opportunity (Bootstrapped Context)

| Rank | Concept | Score | Rationale |
|------|---------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Document Intelligence API | 8.5/10 | Large market, high LTV, sticky product, lower competition at SMB level |
| 2 | AI Resume Screening API | 8/10 | Growing adoption, clear ROI for customers, enterprise potential |
| 3 | AI Lead Enrichment API | 7.5/10 | Established market, many competitors, but room for niche players |
| 4 | Social Media Content Repurposing | 7/10 | Large market, lower barriers, higher churn, more competition |
| 5 | LinkedIn Outreach Automation | 6/10 | Strong demand but high platform risk and policy enforcement |

### Key Success Factors by Concept

**AI Lead Enrichment API:**
- Differentiate on data quality or niche verticals
- Focus on specific use cases (e.g., real estate, SaaS, agencies)
- Competitive on pricing vs. established players

**Document Intelligence API:**
- Superior developer experience vs. AWS/Google
- Pre-built templates for common document types
- Vertical specialization (legal, healthcare, finance)

**Social Media Content Repurposing:**
- Unique AI capabilities for content transformation
- Platform-specific optimization
- Viral growth mechanics in free tier

**LinkedIn Outreach Automation:**
- Compliance-first approach to avoid account bans
- Integration with existing CRMs
- Agency-focused features for multi-account management

**AI Resume Screening API:**
- Focus on SMB market underserved by enterprise solutions
- Bias reduction as competitive advantage
- Integration with popular ATS platforms
